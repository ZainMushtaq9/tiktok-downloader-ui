Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to get a response?

We typically respond within 24-48 hours. During peak times, it may take slightly longer.

Can I report a bug or suggest a feature?

Absolutely! Select "Report a Bug" or "Feature Request" from the subject dropdown and provide details.

How do I report copyright infringement?

Select "Copyright/DMCA" from the subject dropdown and provide detailed information about the infringement. See our Disclaimer for DMCA requirements.

Do you offer technical support?

Yes! Select "Technical Support" and describe your issue. Include your device type, browser, and what platform you're trying to download from.

Business Inquiries

For partnership opportunities, advertising inquiries, or business-related matters, please use the contact form above and select "Partnership/Collaboration" as the subject.

Copyright & DMCA

If you believe your copyrighted work has been infringed through our service, please contact us immediately with:
