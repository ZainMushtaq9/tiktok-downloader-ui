About Us

TikTok Downloader is a free online utility designed to help users download publicly available TikTok videos quickly and easily.

Our platform focuses on simplicity, speed, and user privacy. We do not store any videos on our servers, and we do not require user registration.

This website acts as a technical interface that retrieves content directly from public TikTok URLs provided by users.

We are not affiliated with TikTok or ByteDance in any way.
