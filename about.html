How It Works

Our service uses advanced technology to access publicly available content from social media platforms. Here's how it works:

  1. You paste a video URL from any supported platform
  2. Our servers fetch the video metadata and information
  3. We extract the video file in the best available quality
  4. You download the video directly to your device
  5. We immediately delete all temporary data from our servers

Important: We do not host, store, or redistribute any videos. We are simply a tool that helps you access content that is already publicly available.
