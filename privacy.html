Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect information.

This Privacy Policy explains how Video Downloader Tools ("we", "our", or "us") handles information when you use our website and online tools.

Introduction

Video Downloader Tools is committed to protecting user privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines what information is collected, how it is used, and the choices users have regarding their data.

By accessing or using this website, you agree to the terms described in this Privacy Policy.

Who We Are

Video Downloader Tools is an independent web-based platform that provides tools for downloading publicly available videos from supported social media websites. We do not require users to create accounts or provide personal information to use our tools.

Information We Collect

Video Downloader Tools does not require users to register or provide personally identifiable information in order to use our services.

Like most websites, we may collect limited non-personal information automatically when you visit our website. This information is used solely for website functionality, security, and analytics purposes.

Log Files

Video Downloader Tools follows a standard procedure of using log files. These files log visitors when they visit websites. The information collected by log files may include internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date and time stamps, referring/exit pages, and the number of clicks.

These data points are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable and are used only for analyzing trends, administering the site, tracking users' movement on the website, and gathering demographic information.

Cookies and Web Beacons

Like many websites, Video Downloader Tools uses cookies to store information about visitors’ preferences and to optimize the user experience by customizing web page content based on visitors’ browser type or other information.

Cookies may also be used by third-party advertising partners to display advertisements that are relevant to users based on their interests.

How We Use Information

The information we collect is used to:

The DoubleClick cookie enables Google and its partners to serve ads based on users’ interests and browsing behavior. Users may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting the Google Ads Settings page.

Since Video Downloader Tools does not collect personal information, these rights are generally not applicable. However, users may contact us for clarification regarding data handling practices.
